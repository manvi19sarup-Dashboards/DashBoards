# Food Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of food sales performance across different product categories, channels, regions, and time periods. The dashboard helps identify sales trends, top-performing categories, revenue distribution, and key business insights for data-driven decision-making.

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Track total revenue, orders, and average sales value.
* Compare product category performance.
* Identify top-performing sales channels.
* Monitor quarterly sales trends.
* Support business decisions through interactive visualizations.

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Revenue per Order
* Sales Growth Indicators

### Visualizations

* Revenue by Product Category
* Revenue by Sales Channel
* Quarterly Sales Trend Analysis
* Heat Map for Product Performance
* Interactive Filters and Slicers
* Revenue Distribution Charts

### Filters Available

* Product Category
* Sales Channel
* Quarter
* Region (if applicable)

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Visualization Techniques

## Key Insights

* Identified the highest revenue-generating product categories.
* Analyzed channel-wise sales contribution.
* Evaluated quarterly business performance.
* Highlighted areas requiring sales improvement.
